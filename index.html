Products

Checking

Regular checking at LMPOCU is built to be quick and easy.

Members enrolled can access their funds at any 5/3rd Bank without a service fee.

Also, your debit card can be used at any business that accepts MasterCard!

All of these benefits can be enjoyed without worrying about maintaining a minimum balance!

Saving

We strive to make saving simple for you. We do not require a minimum balance before you start to earn dividends.

Enjoy opening as many linked accounts to help you as you save for diferent life projects.

When you open an account five dollars is held as your membership stake in our credit union.
